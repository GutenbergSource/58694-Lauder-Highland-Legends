This collection of Legends from Scotland was originally published in two volumes in 1837 under the title Highland Rambles and Long Legends to Shorten the Way. This is a 1880 reprint in one volume.

Overall, this is an easy text. Since the source copy was very brittle, a few pages are slightly damaged. Please report these when you encounter them; as I can replace them with alternative scans. In most cases, it will be easy to make out the text, but you could also use the alternative scans to confirm issues.
